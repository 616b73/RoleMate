# RoleMate Project Overview

## Summary
RoleMate is a real-time platform that helps job seekers practice interviews by instantly connecting them with other candidates preparing for similar roles. Instead of relying on personal networks, paid services, or solo preparation, users can join RoleMate, choose the role they are preparing for, and get matched with another candidate for mock interviews, concept discussions, or preparation-focused conversations.

## Problem Statement
Interview preparation is often isolating. Many candidates want to practice with real people but face common constraints:
- they do not know someone preparing for the same role
- friends may not share the same technical background
- paid mock interview services are not always accessible
- scheduling regular practice sessions creates friction

## Core Value Proposition
Anyone preparing for interviews should be able to find a practice partner within seconds. RoleMate reduces the friction involved in finding a partner, coordinating schedules, and repeatedly preparing alone.

## Functionalities & Usefulness for Users
RoleMate applies the concept of random matching to interview preparation. Users select a target role or domain, enter a queue, and get paired with another candidate preparing for the same area. 
Supported roles include Backend Engineering, Frontend Engineering, Full Stack Development, Data Science, DevOps, and UI/UX Design.
Once matched, users can practice technical questions, behavioral rounds, system design discussions, or general interview preparation.
